Pocatello: The Boise of the East

Facts

  • Population: 58,064
  • Year Established: 1889
  • State Region: Southeastern
  • Classification: Urban
  • Average Income: $56,115 (less than Idaho state average by $14,099)

All the Prestige and Glamour Southeast Idaho Can Offer

Pocatello is the fifth-largest city in Idaho and is located in the southeastern part of the state. The city is known for its outdoor activities, including hiking, biking, and fishing. The city is also home to a number of museums, including the Idaho Museum of Natural History and the Bannock County Historical Museum.
