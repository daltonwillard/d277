No Small Potatoes

Idaho is known for its potatoes, but it's also home to a number of other attractions. The state is home to the largest elk population in the United States, and it's also home to the deepest river gorge in North America. The Snake River Canyon is deeper than the Grand Canyon!

Although Idaho is a landlocked state, it has plenty of water. The state is home to over 3,000 rivers and more than 2,000 lakes. The state's largest lake, Lake Pend Oreille, is over 1,100 feet deep.
