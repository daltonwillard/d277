Capital City

Boise is the capital of Idaho and the state's largest city. The city is located in southwestern Idaho, along the Boise River. The city was founded in 1863 during the gold rush, and it was named after the river that runs through it. Boise is known for its outdoor activities, including hiking, biking, and fishing. The city is also home to a number of museums, including the Idaho State Museum and the Boise Art Museum.
