Couer d'Alene is a city in northern Idaho, located along the shores of Lake Couer d'Alene. The city is known for its outdoor activities, including boating, fishing, and hiking. The city is also home to a number of museums, including the Museum of North Idaho and the Couer d'Alene Art Gallery.
